ACREO is airspace+comms+coordination system to manage multiple, simultaneous BVLOS drone operations that can perform is low visibility

Core system = Portable Airspace Management System (PAMS)
- tracks all aircraft
- shares flight intent and position
- real time "situational awareness"
- Alerts operators to conflicts automatically 

ACREO communication chain:
- Drone -> ground station -> PAMS -> other aircraft (PAMS is updated difference)

ACREO doesn't increase raw bandwidth

ACREO Improvements:
- aircraft positions are shared instantly and flight plans synchronized
- operators can see all aircraft positions and flight paths
- better imaging in low visibility conditions
