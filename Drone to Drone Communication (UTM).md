Current UTM comms procedure: 
- 1. Drone to ground control station
- 2. GCS to cloud (used for deconfliction)
- drone to drone used for collision avoidance
Issues:
- BVLOS system rely on 5G/4G networks and areas in which drones are deployed often have little to no coverage
- UTM's cloud reliance has latency, thus delays in position updates and conflict detection occur
