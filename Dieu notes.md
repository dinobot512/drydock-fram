Currently used solution:

	A California system has been up and running since last season. The web site has an integration bar with incidents. The tracking of resources comes from the audio calls and 911. It runs an automatic simulation that shows on a scale of 1 to 5 how complicated the fire is. A new system called JEDI displays wind conditions and impacts on buildings or population. It delivers the information with a one-page report that the firefighters have when they are dispatched and go into the field. It includes tracking of resources, communications, and provides situation awareness.
